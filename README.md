# AI Automation ROI Calculator

🚀 **Live Demo:** https://ke1k01.github.io/ai-roi-calculator/

## About

A free, interactive calculator that helps businesses estimate their return on investment from AI automation. Perfect for:

- Business owners considering automation
- Consultants demonstrating ROI to clients
- Sales teams building business cases
- Anyone evaluating automation investments

## Features

- **Monthly & Annual ROI Calculation** - See immediate and long-term returns
- **Industry-Specific Automation Ideas** - 70+ automation suggestions tailored to your industry
- **Comprehensive Breakdown** - Time savings, revenue uplift, error reduction
- **Print-Friendly Reports** - Generate professional PDF reports
- **Mobile Responsive** - Works on any device

## Supported Industries

- Marketing Agencies
- Professional Services
- E-Commerce / Retail
- SaaS / Tech
- Consulting
- Healthcare
- General Business

## How to Use

1. Enter your business metrics (revenue, employees, hourly wage)
2. Adjust automation parameters (hours saved, investment cost)
3. Click "Calculate My ROI" to see results
4. Print or save as PDF for presentations

## Monetization Ideas

This calculator can be used as:
- **Lead Magnet** - Collect emails before showing full results
- **Consulting Tool** - Demonstrate value to prospects
- **Affiliate Content** - Link to automation tools and services
- **Content Marketing** - Embed on blog posts about automation
